Mars

Weather provided by NASA's Mars lander InSight

InSight is the first lander on Mars with a mission to explore the deep interior of the planet. It is located in the Elysium Planitia Region of Mars. The objective is to investigate the processes that shaped the rocky planets of the inner solar system.
